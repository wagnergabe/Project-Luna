Project Luna

Helping Special Pets find Homes

Project Luna is dedicated to connecting special needs pets with loving homes.

This app serves as a bridge between special needs pets in shelters and rescue organizations and individuals looking to provide them with a forever home. Whether it's a senior dog with mobility issues, a cat with a chronic medical condition, or a rabbit with special dietary needs, Project Luna is here to match these extraordinary animals with compassionate guardians who are ready to offer them the care and attention they deserve.
